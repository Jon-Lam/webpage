OUR AGENTS

Alex Verigo

Alex Vergo is President of Platinum Travel, and enjoys putting together marketing plans and working on this very website. Past positions at Holland America Line and The Walt Disney Company gave him lots of experience in working with a wide array of clients and diverse destinations. His favorite destinations include Alaska and Eastern Europe, with his most favorite being Australia (he's an Australia Specialist through Tourism Australia). If you're ever in the market for a phone call from Mickey Mouse, he does a great impersonation!

Betty Sara

Betty Sarah is a Luxury Travel Advisor as well as Vice-President at Platinum Travel. She brings a world of experience and a vast array of knowledge to the Platinum Travel staff. Emily holds degrees in Travel and Tourism and Hospitality Management. Emily's career background in both the leisure and corporate travel industry has led her to gain accreditation for various organizations. In 2010, she was appointed by Travel Agent Magazine as one of the Top 30 Travel Advisors under the Age of 30 and in 2013 was selected as part of the Virtuoso Travel Advisory board for Islands Magazine, Caribbean Travel and Life, and Destination Weddings and Honeymoons.

Carla Sanchez

Carla Sanchez has been with Platinum Travel specializing in luxury adventure travel and romantic escapes for over 15 years, and has been a travel consultant for nearly 25 years. She loves to travel, and really enjoys sitting down with clients and planning the perfect trip for them. Growing us as an Air Force "brat", she was exposed to many different cultures and destinations while living all over the U.S. and South Pacific, and, as a result of those experiences, has remained eager for new travel adventures. She would love to help you plan your own adventures and memories for a true "Return on Life".
